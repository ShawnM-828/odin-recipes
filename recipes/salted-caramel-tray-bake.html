Salted Caramel Tray Bake

Description

This delicious, simple cake is full of golden, caramel flavours, from the brown sugar in the batter to the salted caramel sauce in the icing. Yum!

Ingredients

  • 180g butter
  • 1/4 cup plain flour
  • 1/4 cup caster sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence
  • 1 1/2 cups self raising flour
  • 1/2 cup sour cream or plain yoghurt
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup caramel sauce
  • 3/4 tsp sea salt flakes

For Icing

  • 100g butter, softened
  • 1 1/3 cup icing sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180C. Line an 18cm x 25cm slice tin with baking paper.
  2. In a stand mixer or using hand-held beaters, cream butter and sugars until thick. Add eggs one at a time, beating well between each addition. Beat in vanilla. Add flour, sour cream and salt and mix until just combined. Pour mixture into prepared tin and bake for 40-45 minutes until a skewer inserted into the centre comes out clean. Allow to cool in tin.
  3. Make icing by whisking together softened butter and sugar until smooth. Combine caramel sauce with salt flakes and add 1/2 cup to the icing. Beat well to combine.
  4. Spread caramel icing over cooled cake. Drizzle with remaining salted caramel sauce. Slice into squares and serve.

Cook's Notes

You can use store-bought caramel sauce or make your own.
